Introduction

Human endogenous retroviruses and other LTR type retrotransposons (HERV/LRs) have regulatory elements possibly affecting on transcriptions of host genes. dbHERV-REs is a database for these regulatory elements of HERV/LRs. The dbHERV-REs provides following information; (i) HERV-TFBSs, overlaps between HERV/LRs and transcription factor binding sites (TFBSs) determined by ChIP-Seq with 100 transcription factors (TFs), (ii) HERV/LRs Conserved Regulatory Elements (HCREs), TF-binding motifs in HERV-TFBSs that are conserved among quite a few copies of a type of HERV/LRs, (iii) HERV-DHSs, overlaps between HERV/LRs and DNase I hypersensitive sites (DHSs), an indicator of chromosome free regions, and (iv) Gene Ontology (GO) terms in which insertions of a kind of HCREs were enriched. Additionally, in order to approach evolutionary dynamics of regulatory elements that occurred in the course of genomic expansion of the HERV type, this database can compare phylogenetic tree of copies of a HERV/LR type with presence or absence of TFBSs, TF-binding motifs, and orthologous copies in genome of mammalians. Further descriptions are written in our paper [REF].
